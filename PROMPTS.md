# Claude AI Prompts

## Resume Generation
- "Create a resume for a [job title] with [X years] of experience in [industry] and skills in [skill 1, skill 2, skill 3]."
- "Generate a resume summary for someone transitioning from [old industry/position] to [new industry/position]."

## Cover Letter Generation
- "Draft a cover letter for a [job title] position at [company name], emphasizing my experience in [relevant experience] and my passion for [industry/field]."
- "Write a cover letter to accompany my application for [specific role], highlighting my skills in [skill 1] and [skill 2]."

## Cold Email
- "Compose a cold email to [recipient's name] at [company] requesting an informational interview about opportunities in [specific field]."
- "Write a cold outreach email pitching my services in [specific service] to [prospective client]."

## Interview Questions
- "What are some common interview questions for a [job title] position?"
- "Generate a list of behavioral interview questions suitable for [job title] candidates."

## Talking Points
- "Create talking points for an interview for a [job title] role, focusing on my strengths in [strength 1, strength 2]."
- "List key talking points to discuss during an informational interview in [industry/field]."

## Skills Gap Analysis
- "Analyze the skills required for a [job title] position compared to my current skills."
- "What skills should I develop to transition from [current position] to [desired position]?"
