# TIER3 INTERVIEW PREP

## Skills Gap Analysis
- Identify the key skills required for TIER 3 roles in tech and assess your proficiency against them.
- Create a chart of your skills vs. job requirements.

## Interview Questions
1. Describe a time when you had to troubleshoot a challenging technical issue. What was the outcome?
2. Explain the importance of automated testing and share your experience with it.
3. How do you approach learning a new technology or programming language?

## Talking Points
- Be prepared to discuss your most significant projects in detail.
- Highlight your understanding of industry trends and technologies related to the role.
- Demonstrate your problem-solving abilities and how you've applied them.

## Salary Negotiation Guide
- Research industry standards for TIER 3 roles.
- Consider the total compensation package (salary, benefits, bonuses).
- Practice your negotiation skills with a friend or mentor before the interview.
