# TIER 2 COLD OUTREACH

## Cold Email Templates

### Template 1: Introduction Email
Subject: Introduction & Potential Collaboration

Hello [Recipient's Name],

I hope this message finds you well! My name is [Your Name], and I am reaching out to introduce myself and explore potential opportunities for collaboration. I have a keen interest in [specific area related to the recipient's company/field]. 

Looking forward to connecting!

Best,
[Your Name]

---

### Template 2: Follow-Up Email
Subject: Following Up on My Previous Email

Hi [Recipient's Name],

I wanted to follow up on my previous message regarding potential collaboration opportunities. I believe there could be significant synergy between our efforts.

Would you be open to a short call this week?

Best regards,
[Your Name]

## Personal Angle
When reaching out, consider personalizing your message based on:
- Common interests
- Recent industry events
- Mutual connections or alumni from the same schools

Be genuine and let your passion show!
