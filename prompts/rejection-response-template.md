# Rejection Response Templates

Gracious rejection responses that keep doors open and build professional relationships.

---

## Template 1: Standard Rejection Response

**Subject:** Re: [JOB_TITLE] Application — Thank You

```
Hi [HIRING_MANAGER_NAME / Recruiter Name],

Thank you for letting me know about the [JOB_TITLE] decision, and for the time your team invested in reviewing my application.

I have a lot of respect for [COMPANY_NAME] and the work you're doing in [INDUSTRY/PRODUCT_AREA], and I hope our paths cross again in the future.

If you're open to it, I'd genuinely appreciate any feedback on my application — it would help me grow and improve for future opportunities.

Wishing you and the team continued success.

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
[LINKEDIN_URL]
```

## Template 2: After Reaching Final Round

**Subject:** Re: [JOB_TITLE] — Thank You for the Experience

```
Hi [HIRING_MANAGER_NAME],

Thank you for sharing the decision regarding the [JOB_TITLE] role. While I'm disappointed not to be joining the team, I truly valued the entire interview process.

The conversations I had with [INTERVIEWER_NAMES] were some of the most engaging and thought-provoking I've had. I learned a great deal about [SPECIFIC_CHALLENGE_OR_TOPIC_DISCUSSED].

I'd love to stay connected on LinkedIn and keep an eye on future opportunities at [COMPANY_NAME]. Is there anything specific you'd recommend I focus on to be a stronger candidate in the future?

Thank you again — I hope to cross paths with you all down the road.

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]
[LINKEDIN_URL]
```

## Template 3: Requesting Feedback

**Subject:** Feedback Request — [JOB_TITLE] Application

```
Hi [HIRING_MANAGER_NAME],

Thank you for your email regarding the [JOB_TITLE] position. I appreciate you taking the time to inform me.

I completely understand, and I respect the decision. I'm committed to continuous improvement and would be grateful for any specific feedback you're able to share about:
- Where my application or interview fell short
- Skills or experiences that would have made me a stronger candidate
- Any advice for future applications at [COMPANY_NAME]

Even a brief response would be incredibly valuable. Thank you for any time you're able to give.

Warm regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
```

## Response Best Practices

### DO:
- Respond within 24–48 hours of receiving the rejection
- Express genuine gratitude (not just politeness)
- Reference something specific about the company or process
- Ask for feedback (most won't respond, but some will — it's gold)
- Connect on LinkedIn to stay on their radar

### DON'T:
- Express bitterness or frustration
- Ask why they chose someone else over you
- Write a long, emotional response
- Burn the bridge — people move companies and roles frequently

### Timing After Rejection

| Action | Timing |
|---|---|
| Send gracious reply | Within 24–48 hours |
| Send LinkedIn connection | Same day as reply |
| Follow up for feedback (if no response) | 1 week after reply |
| Reapply to same company (different role) | 3–6 months (or sooner if it's a different team/role) |
