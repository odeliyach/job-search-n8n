# Follow-up Email Templates

## Overview

These templates are used by the follow-up scheduler workflow to generate personalized follow-up emails. All emails MUST be approved by the user before sending.

---

## Template 1: Day 3 Follow-up (After Application)

**Subject:** Following up on my application - `{{job_title}}` at `{{company_name}}`

**Body:**
Hi [Hiring Manager Name],

I wanted to follow up on my application for the `{{job_title}}` position I submitted on `{{applied_date}}`.

I'm genuinely excited about this opportunity — [specific reason related to company]. I've been following [specific company achievement/product] and believe my experience with [specific skill] would be a strong fit.

Would you be able to share a timeline for next steps?

Best,
Odeliya Charitonova
odeliya.charitonova.dev@gmail.com

## Usage Notes

- All templates are STARTING POINTS — the AI will personalize based on context
- Variables in `{{double_braces}}` are replaced automatically
- Variables in `[square brackets]` require manual personalization or AI generation
- **ZERO emails are sent without manual user approval**
- User reviews in N8N interface before any email is dispatched
