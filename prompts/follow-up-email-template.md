# Follow-Up Email Templates

Use these templates for follow-up outreach after submitting job applications.
Customize the `[PLACEHOLDERS]` with real data before sending.

---

## Template 1: One-Week Follow-Up (After Application)

**Subject:** Following up — [JOB_TITLE] Application at [COMPANY_NAME]

```
Hi [HIRING_MANAGER_NAME / Hiring Team],

I wanted to follow up on my application for the [JOB_TITLE] position submitted on [APPLICATION_DATE].

I'm genuinely excited about [COMPANY_NAME]'s work on [SPECIFIC_PRODUCT_OR_INITIATIVE] and believe my experience with [RELEVANT_SKILL_1] and [RELEVANT_SKILL_2] would add real value to your team.

Would you have 15 minutes this week to connect? I'm happy to work around your schedule.

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
[LINKEDIN_URL] | [PORTFOLIO_URL]
```

---

## Template 2: Post-Interview Thank You

**Subject:** Thank you — [JOB_TITLE] Interview at [COMPANY_NAME]

```
Hi [INTERVIEWER_NAME],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today about the [JOB_TITLE] role.

I especially enjoyed our conversation about [SPECIFIC_TOPIC_DISCUSSED]. It reinforced my excitement about [COMPANY_NAME]'s direction on [PRODUCT_OR_CHALLENGE].

As mentioned, I've attached [RELEVANT_PORTFOLIO_ITEM] that directly relates to the challenge you described regarding [PROBLEM_MENTIONED].

Looking forward to the next steps. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything else from me.

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]
```

## Template 3: Two-Week Follow-Up (No Response)

**Subject:** Re: [JOB_TITLE] at [COMPANY_NAME] — Still Very Interested

```
Hi [HIRING_MANAGER_NAME],

I hope you're doing well. I wanted to touch base once more regarding the [JOB_TITLE] position.

Since my last message, I've [NEW_ACHIEVEMENT_OR_RELEVANT_UPDATE — e.g., "completed a relevant project" or "published an article on X"].

I remain very enthusiastic about the opportunity and would love to connect at your convenience.

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
```

---

## Template 4: After Rejection — Keep the Door Open

**Subject:** Re: [JOB_TITLE] Application — Thank You

```
Hi [HIRING_MANAGER_NAME],

Thank you for letting me know about the [JOB_TITLE] decision. While I'm disappointed, I truly appreciate the time your team invested in reviewing my application.

I remain a big admirer of [COMPANY_NAME]'s work, and I'd love to stay connected for future opportunities that might be a better fit.

If you're open to it, I'd welcome any feedback on my application to help me improve.

Thank you again,
[YOUR_NAME]
[LINKEDIN_URL]
```

---

## Timing Guide

| Scenario | Wait Before Following Up |
|---|---|
| After submitting application | 5–7 business days |
| After phone screen | 24–48 hours (send thank you) |
| After on-site/final interview | 24 hours (send thank you) |
| No response to follow-up | 7–10 business days |
| After rejection | Immediately (gracious response) |

## Placeholders Reference

| Placeholder | Where to Find It |
|---|---|
| `[COMPANY_NAME]` | Job application data |
| `[JOB_TITLE]` | Job application data |
| `[HIRING_MANAGER_NAME]` | LinkedIn, company website, job posting |
| `[APPLICATION_DATE]` | Your application tracking system |
| `[SPECIFIC_PRODUCT_OR_INITIATIVE]` | Company research (from Tier 2) |
| `[RELEVANT_SKILL_1/2]` | Your resume / job requirements match |
| `[SPECIFIC_TOPIC_DISCUSSED]` | Your interview notes |
